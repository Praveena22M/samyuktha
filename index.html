About Us

Welcome to Samyuktha Scans!

Anxious about your pregnancy or worried about your abdomen pain? Come to us immediately and get yourself diagnosed correctly for reassurance and peace of mind. Our team of expert Radiologists, Sonologists and other technicians will take you thro the process of diagnosis in a calm and friendly manner.

Our CT Scan facility has unique technology edge for very precise imaging that assists for correct diagnosis. Our special 4D scans – A unique opportunity for you and your family to see and bond with your baby even before he/she is born!

We offer follow-up ultrasound scans for follicle tracking, ovulation and assessment of the endometrium. Our Comprehensive Ultrasound Scan Package includes Complete OB screening, Gallbladder, abdomen, biliary tract abnormalities, Evaluative imaging before and after liver, Pancreas and Kidney. Come to us and we assure you complete peace of mind through correct diagnosis.
