About Samyuktha Scans!

Samyuktha Scans, a health care initiative of Smart Ventures, has diversified business establishments on Online Retails, Listing Classified Portals and Medical Diagnostics.

We have our presence in medical services for the past 18 years, and into the Medical Diagnostics for the past 4 years serving the needy segment of the society. At present we are located at Alwarthirunagar, which has an easy approach and well connected by road, from all main places. As a part of our expansion strategies, we have plans to have our presence in various parts of Chennai and also in southern districts of Tamil Nadu in years to come. We are in the process of getting our Diagnostic Services certified by bodies like ISO and NABL.

We are associated with various Corporate Clients in Hotel Industry, Software Domain, Manufacturing Companies, TV and Print Media, Chinnathirai Nadigar Sangam, Educational Institutions/ Colleges, Banks, Telecom companies, Construction Industries, Security agencies in their Pre- Employment Checkups, Food handlers Examination, Executive Health Checkups and Master Health Checkups. We have ambulance facilities for pick up and drop for needy / emergency patients. We operate 24X7 in a week, and cover round the clock for 24 Hours
